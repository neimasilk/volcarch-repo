# E039 Trilogy: VCS Constraint for P11

**Date:** 2026-03-11
**Status:** 3 sub-tests complete. All INFORMATIVE NEGATIVE at global scale.
**Implication:** P11 must scope VCS claims to local Java/Bali scale.

---

## Three Tests, One Conclusion

| Test | Method | Result | Direction |
|------|--------|--------|-----------|
| E039a (binary) | Pulotu Q32 island type: volcanic (61) vs non-volcanic (76) | p=0.973 | REVERSED |
| E039b (distance) | Haversine distance to nearest of 854 GVP volcanoes | rho=+0.145, p=0.092 | OPPOSITE |
| E039c (subsistence) | 9 Pulotu variables (cooperation, authority, subsistence) | 2/9 sig, but = fertility | MIXED |

**VCS as global Austronesian phenomenon: REJECTED.**

## What E039c Actually Found

Group hunting (rho=-0.275, p=0.002) and population (rho=-0.226, p=0.010) correlate with volcanic proximity. But eruption count correlates with agriculture (rho=-0.248, p=0.004). This is **volcanic soil fertility** driving demographics, not cultural selection for ritual/cooperation.

## What P11 CAN Still Claim

1. VCS at **local scale** (specific volcanic complexes): E031 (candi siting, p<0.0001) and E032 (Pranata Mangsa × eruption seasonality, p=0.042) remain valid within Java.
2. VCS as **volcanic informedness** (where to build, when to plant), not as ritual complexity amplifier.
3. The Q21 mana signal (p=0.006) — volcanic cultures score higher on "spiritual power/energy" concept. Doesn't survive Bonferroni but worth noting.

## What P11 CANNOT Claim

- "Volcanic cultures have higher ritual complexity globally" — FALSE
- "Living on a volcanic island produces more elaborate ritual" — FALSE
- "VCS is a pan-Austronesian phenomenon" — REJECTED
