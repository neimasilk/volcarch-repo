## Hypothesis

Coastal taphonomy (erosion + submersion post-LGM 120m sea-level rise) systematically destroys maritime archaeological records in Nusantara. The Liang Metanduno cave art (67,800 BP) on Muna Island demonstrates human presence far predating surviving coastal evidence.

- Bathymetric surveys of Sunda Shelf
