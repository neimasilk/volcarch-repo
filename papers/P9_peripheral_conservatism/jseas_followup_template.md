## Backup Journal: Archipel (CNRS)

**Journal:** Archipel — Études interdisciplinaires sur le monde insulindien
**Publisher:** CNRS / Association Archipel (Paris)
**ISSN:** 0044-8613 (print), 2104-3655 (online)
**Scopus:** Yes (Q2 in Cultural Studies, History)
**APC:** FREE (Diamond OA since 2019, on OpenEdition Journals)
**Language:** French OR English (both accepted)
**Scope:** Interdisciplinary studies on the Malay-Indonesian world — history, anthropology, linguistics, archaeology, literature
**Review:** Double-blind peer review
**Typical turnaround:** 3-6 months
**Website:** https://journals.openedition.org/archipel/
**Submission:** Email to archipel@ehess.fr

### Why Archipel is a good backup:
1. **Free** (Diamond OA, CNRS funding)
2. **Indonesia-focused** — Malagasy-Nusantaran comparison is within scope
3. **Interdisciplinary** — linguistic + botanical + ritual evidence fits perfectly
4. **English accepted** — no translation needed
5. **Q2 Scopus** — comparable to JSEAS ranking

### Adaptation needed for Archipel:
- JSEAS uses footnote citations (biblatex verbose-note) → Archipel uses **author-date** (Chicago style)
- JSEAS British English → Archipel accepts either British or American
- Word limit: Archipel allows up to ~15,000 words (P9 is ~10,100 — no issue)
- Anonymous version still needed (double-blind)
- May need to add brief French abstract (1 paragraph)

### Second backup: Indonesia and the Malay World (Routledge)

- **APC:** Varies (some OA options, ~$2,600 for gold OA). Standard publication = free.
- **Scope:** Southeast Asian studies, Malaysia/Indonesia focus
- **Review:** Single-blind
- **Advantage:** Routledge prestige, wider readership
- **Disadvantage:** Not Diamond OA; if revision needed, slower turnaround
