# E059: Priority Fieldwork Targets — Where to Dig Next

## Purpose
Synthesize P2 settlement model, E031 candi data, E051 toponymic classification,
and volcanic geology to identify the TOP 10 most promising locations for finding
buried pre-Hindu archaeological sites in Java.

## Method
Generated 288 candidate points around 8 major Java volcanoes at 8-15km distance.
Scored each by: (1) estimated burial depth, (2) nearby candi count within 10km,
(3) court-center activity (Sanskrit toponymic ratio), (4) volcanic proximity.

## Results

### Top Target: Blitar-Kelud Zone
ALL top 10 targets cluster around **Gunung Kelud** (East Java):
- Kelud sedimentation rate: **13.1 mm/yr** (highest measured)
- Pre-Hindu sites at 400 CE would be buried under **21+ meters** of tephra
- **6-7 candi within 10 km** (proven historical occupation)
- Kabupaten pre-Hindu ratio: 18-50% (moderate Indianization = important area)
