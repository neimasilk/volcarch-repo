# E060: Pre-400 CE Nusantara Reconstruction

## Purpose
Synthesize ALL VOLCARCH evidence channels into a single coherent reconstruction
of what existed in Nusantara before the conventional "start" at 400 CE.

## Method
Compiled findings from 54 experiments across 8 independent evidence channels:
1. Linguistic Reconstruction (PMP vocabulary, ABVD)
2. Material Culture/Epigraphy (DHARMA corpus, E023/E035/E040)
3. Genetic Evidence (aDNA, E053)
4. Botanical Evidence (Canarium, E044/E050)
5. Comparative Ethnography (Madagascar, Trunyan, Pulotu)
6. Geographic/Toponymic (E051, BPS data)
7. Architectural (E031, E056)
8. Geological/Taphonomic (P1/P9, E052, E053)

Reconstructed 6 domains: Economy, Religion, Settlement, Technology, Political, Script.

## Results
